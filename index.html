Who Am I?

AKA Why Hire Me?

I love how challenging and interactive Frontend development is. As an eager and deeply-motivated Frontend Developer with a strong technical skill-set and good communication skills, I want to use that enthusiasm to impact and elevate the company that I work for. I want to take the user experience to the next level through technical design.

In addition to bringing passion to code, I bring passion and ideas to the human/social side of engineering (which I believe is very important and often over-looked). I am good at motivating and helping my team, asking questions (whether for personal guidance or to help define a product), and translating engineering ideas into useful information for non-technical coworkers.

I am currently looking for a full-time position as a front-end developer in San Francisco.
